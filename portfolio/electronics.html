Audio Equalizer

An audio equalizer with three different bandpass filters. Each band had an adjustable gain that created a noticable output difference when changed. Music was played through the design and the indiviual bands adjusted treble, midrange, and bass, of the music. The design was a group project, however, I ended up doing most of the work which included designing the circuit and running simulations using PSpice.

A full design paper for the project can be downloaded below. It includes multiple sections: An abstract, introduction, theory, design, procedures, results and discussion, and conclusion.
